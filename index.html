What to Expect During Your DOT Physical

Most exams take just 30–45 minutes from start to finish.

1

Medical History Review

We review your health history, prescriptions, and vital signs including height, weight, and blood pressure.

2

Vision & Hearing Tests

Quick screenings to ensure you meet minimum standards; bring your glasses or hearing aids if you use them.

3

Full Physical Exam

Blood pressure, pulse, urinalysis, and an overall assessment of your fitness to safely drive a commercial vehicle.

4

Same-Day Certification

If you pass, you receive your Medical Examiner’s Certificate on the spot, and we submit your information electronically to the FMCSA.

What to Bring to Your Appointment

  • Valid driver’s license or photo ID
  • List of all current medications and dosages
  • Glasses or contact lenses, if you wear them
  • Hearing aids, if you use them
  • Contact information for other doctors you see
  • Relevant medical records (diabetes, sleep apnea, etc.)
  • A full bladder for urinalysis
